How to Play

Basics

  • Pick a weapon on the left and right to set up a 1v1 in Classic.
  • Press Play to start or pause. Spacebar also toggles Play/Pause.
  • Use - and + to zoom the whole game view.
  • Toggle Gravity with the Gravity button (on by default in Classic).
  • Drag a ball on the arena to reposition it before or during a match.

Controls

  • Play/Pause: Click Play or press Spacebar
  • Reset: Resets the match while keeping selections
  • Zoom: Use the - and + buttons under Play/Reset
  • Hide/Show UI: Hide UI hides sidebars and controls. Show UI restores everything except the Boss sidebar in Boss mode (it stays hidden).
  • Horizontal Velocity: Toggle to allow or suppress horizontal movement (affects all balls).
  • Drag: Click/touch and hold a ball to move it; release to restore its momentum

Gamemodes

  • Classic: 1v1 duel. Gravity ON by default.
  • Free for All: Add up to 30 fighters. Pick any mix of weapons.
  • Teams: Blue vs Red squads. Add fighters to each side.
  • Boss Fight: A single giant Red boss with 5000 HP vs Blue team. Slower, much larger. Available only on Large or Huge arenas.

Arena Sizes

  • Small / Medium / Large / Huge: Pick from Gamemodes menu.
  • Shrinking (FFA/Teams only): Starts Huge and shrinks over time. The shrinking begins after you press Play.

Weapons

  • Basic: A simple ball with no weapon.
  • Sword: Increases damage on hit; can parry; grants brief immunity on hit.
  • Dagger: Very fast spinner; short mini-cooldown between hits.
  • Spear: Grows damage and range with each hit.
  • Bow: Fires arrows that accelerate as more are active; bow and arrow tips can be parried/deflected.
  • Scythe: On hit, applies 1 poison stack to the opponent. Every 5 seconds, poison deals damage equal to current stacks and makes the opponent flash purple.
  • Shuriken: Throws bouncing stars; gains more bounces on hit; projectiles can be deflected.
  • Unarmed: Your body is the weapon; charges speed over time and slams into foes.
  • Crusher: No separate weapon. A heavy, oversized ball that can instantly crush enemies against arena walls or corners when pressing them into the edge.
  • Grower: Slowly grows in size; contact damage scales up. At max size it explodes, heavily damaging nearby foes.
  • Shrinker: Gradually shrinks, becoming faster; contact damage scales with size. Shrinks to 0 to pop and die.
  • Axe: Heavy hit applies a short stun and a bleed that deals periodic damage for several seconds.
  • Staff: Cycles magic modes (forcefield, expand, shrink, shadow, poison) that change defense, size, speed, and on-hit effects.
  • SpeedBall: No external weapon; gains speed on wall bounces. At high speed it periodically zaps the nearest enemy with lightning.
  • Duplicator: Duplicates on wall bounces (clones are basic, smaller, and capped in total count).
  • Dummy Ball: A training ball with infinite health.
  • AK‑47: Spins and fires fast bullets; bullets vanish on hit and can be parried/deflected by some weapons.
  • Fuse: Combines two weapons into one; both sub-weapons act together and their effects stack conservatively.
  • Custom (AI): Use AI Maker to generate and save your own melee or projectile weapon, complete with an AI icon and gallery sharing.

Tips

  • Parries occur when two weapons meet while facing each other.
  • Projectiles can be deflected by weapon collisions.
  • Try different arena sizes and gravity settings for varied gameplay.

Customizer

Tips:
  • Auto target adapts to your current mode.
  • Radius updates mass automatically. Some weapons may further adjust speed.
  • Boss health text scales with size; large values are OK.
